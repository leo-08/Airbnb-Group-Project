OUR PROJECT¶

Airbnb is a platform that lists private accommodations for rental. It connects people who want to rent out their homes with others who are looking for an accommodation in that location.¶

Our Project focuses on an empirical analysis of Airbnb listings in the US in order to determine price drivers. We obtained the dataset for those Airbnb listings from kaggle. There were several steps involved to make the data usable and derive insights from it.¶

airbnb['amenities_count'] = airbnb['amenities'].str.count(',') + 1

MODEL LIMITATIONS¶

Although the outcome of our work yields some eminently sensible results, we must acknowledge the low r-squared and high RMSE. Clearly, model is lacking certain key criteria that would help it better predict the price.¶

When considering why this may be, we conclude that there are more factors that need to be considered. Here, we draw from our personal experiences as travellers and Airbnb users to suggest that the relative location of a listing probably has a major impact on its price. By relative location, we mean being positioned in close proximity to something that enables it to serve a higher purpose than simply that of accommodation. For instance, a flat located near the airport will offer more value to business travellers looking to jet in and out of a city. Interestingly, we found other projects that subsequently validates our assumption (Celekli, 2018).¶
